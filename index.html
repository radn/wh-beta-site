Upcoming events

At The Washington Harbour there’s always something going on. Come for the live entertainment, exquisite dining experiences, and outdoor fun all year round. In November, the fountain transforms into the Washington Harbour Ice Rink, open every day for public skating, until March. At almost 12,000 square feet, it’s DC’s largest outdoor rink, and offers plenty of special activities for all ages every week.
